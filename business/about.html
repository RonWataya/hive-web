About MoneyHive

Empowering Financial Access Globally

Welcome to MoneyHive, a cutting-edge global financial access platform that serves as the bridge connecting businesses and individuals seeking loans and capital with a vast network of lenders, creditors, and investors. At MoneyHive, we believe in fostering financial inclusion and empowerment by providing a seamless and secure space for financial transactions.

A Proud Malawian Product

MoneyHive proudly hails from the warm heart of Africa, as it is a product developed under Miwallet, a pioneering financial technology company based in Malawi. Miwallet, co-founded by the visionary Ronnex Wataya, has been at the forefront of revolutionizing financial services in the region.

Founder

Ronnex Wataya, the founder of MoneyHive and co-founder of Miwalletmw Limited, is a dynamic entrepreneur and software engineer with a passion for leveraging technology to enhance financial access. With a wealth of experience in the financial technology sector, Wataya has led the development of MoneyHive with a vision to create a global platform that empowers businesses and individuals alike.

Facilitating Financial Connections

MoneyHive is more than just a lending platform; it's a dynamic ecosystem where financial connections are forged, opportunities are unlocked, and dreams are realized. Whether you are a small business seeking capital to grow or an individual looking for a personal loan, MoneyHive provides a user-friendly platform to connect you with the right lenders and investors.
